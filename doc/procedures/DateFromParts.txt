.procedure DateFromParts
.category Dates and Times
.description
Creates a t[Date] for the specified year, month, and day.
.overload
.parameter year: Number
Four-digit year.
.parameter month: Number
Month (1-12).
.parameter day: Number
Day (1-31).
.return Date
A new t[Date].
.example
.example-code
dim x = DateFromParts(2021, 3, 12)
x.Print
.example-output
2021-03-12
.
