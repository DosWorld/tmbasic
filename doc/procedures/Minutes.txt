.procedure Minutes
.category Dates and Times
.description
Creates a t[TimeSpan] for the specified number of minutes.
.overload
.parameter count: Number
The number of minutes in the time span.
.return TimeSpan
A time span representing i[count] minutes.
.example
.example-code
dim x = Minutes(1)
x.Print
x = x + Minutes(0.5)
x.Print
.example-output
00:01:00
00:01:30
.
