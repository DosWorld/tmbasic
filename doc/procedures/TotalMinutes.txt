.procedure TotalMinutes
.category Dates and Times
.description
Converts a t[TimeSpan] to minutes.
.overload
.parameter span: TimeSpan
The time span to convert.
.return Number
A possibly fractional number of minutes.
.example
.example-code
dim x = Seconds(90)
x.TotalMinutes.Print
.example-output
1.5
.
