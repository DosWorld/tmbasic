.procedure Seconds
.category Dates and Times
.description
Creates a t[TimeSpan] for the specified number of seconds.
.overload
.parameter count: Number
The number of seconds in the time span.
.return TimeSpan
A time span representing i[count] seconds.
.example
.example-code
dim x = Seconds(1)
x.Print
x = x + Seconds(0.5)
x.Print
.example-output
00:00:01
00:00:01.5
.
