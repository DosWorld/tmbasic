.procedure Days
.category Dates and Times
.description
Creates a t[TimeSpan] for the specified number of days.
.overload
.parameter count: Number
The number of days in the time span.
.return TimeSpan
A time span representing i[count] days.
.example
.example-code
dim x = Days(1)
x.Print
x = x + Days(0.5)
x.Print
.example-output
1.00:00:00
1.12:00:00
.
