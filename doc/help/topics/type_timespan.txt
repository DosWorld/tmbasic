h1[`TimeSpan` Type]

`TimeSpan` represents a duration of time with millisecond precision.

Use the functions p[Milliseconds], p[Seconds], p[Minutes], p[Hours], or p[Days] to create a `TimeSpan`.

Use the functions p[TotalMilliseconds], p[TotalSeconds], p[TotalMinutes], p[TotalHours], or p[TotalDays]
to retrieve the duration in the desired time units.
