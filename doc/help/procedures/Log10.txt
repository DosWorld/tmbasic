.procedure Log10
.category Algebra and Trigonometry
.blurb Calculates the base-10 logarithm of a number.
.description
Returns the base-10 logarithm of a number.
Use the p[Log] function for natural (base-e) logarithms.
.overload
.parameter x: Number
Input number.
.return Number
Base-10 logarithm of i[x].
.example
.example-code
print Log10(1000)
.example-output
3
.
