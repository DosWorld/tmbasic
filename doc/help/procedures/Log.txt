.procedure Log
.category Algebra and Trigonometry
.blurb Calculates the natural (base-e) logarithm of a number.
.description
Returns the natural (base-e) logarithm of a number.
Use the p[Log10] function for base-10 logarithms.

The built-in constant `EULER` contains the value of the mathematical constant i[e].
.overload
.parameter x: Number
Input number.
.return Number
Base-e logarithm of i[x].
.example
.example-code
print Log(EULER)
.example-output
1
.
