.procedure Min
.category Math
.blurb Returns the smaller of two numbers.
.description
Returns the smaller of two numbers.

If both numbers are equal, either one is returned.
.overload
.parameter x: Number
First number to compare.
.parameter y: Number
Second number to compare.
.return Number
The smaller of i[x] and i[y].
.example
.example-code
print Min(5, 3)
print Min(-2, 1)
.example-output
3
-2
